# Elevation

## Purpose

Elevation is intentionally subtle.

The Jewelett UI should feel layered and tactile without drifting into glossy consumer-app effects.

## Levels

- `elevation.surface.rest`
- `elevation.surface.raised`
- `elevation.surface.floating`
- `elevation.surface.overlay`

## Usage

- `rest` for cards on calm canvases
- `raised` for product tiles or controls that need slight separation
- `floating` for temporary surfaces and richer merchandising modules
- `overlay` for modal or sheet-like surfaces

## Figma note

Elevation tokens are part of the semantic system, but they are not directly storable as native Variables in Figma.

Mirror them through effect styles or documentation rather than trying to force them into the Variables panel.
